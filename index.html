The memory layer
Most AI tools forget you exist between sessions.

ReefAgent has a three-layer memory system that compounds with every interaction. Your preferences, your decisions, your codebase patterns — it all stacks. Your 100th conversation is exponentially more valuable than your first.

Episodic — what happened (conversations, decisions, outcomes)
Semantic — what it means (extracted knowledge, connections)
Procedural — how to do things (learned skills, patterns)
