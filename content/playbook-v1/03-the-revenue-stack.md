# Chapter 3: The Revenue Stack

---

One revenue stream is a job. Five revenue streams stacked on top of each other is a business.

This chapter maps the five layers of AI agent revenue -- from the fastest to ship (content) to the most ambitious (bot-to-bot commerce). Each layer funds the next.

---

## The Five Layers

**Layer 1: Content That Sells** -- Playbooks, guides, templates
**Layer 2: Services That Scale** -- Managed AI employees
**Layer 3: Infrastructure That Compounds** -- Cloud-hosted agents
**Layer 4: Marketplace That Networks** -- Agent and skill marketplace
**Layer 5: Economy That Self-Sustains** -- Bot-to-bot commerce

---

## Layer 1: Content That Sells

**What:** Digital products -- playbooks, templates, configs, guides -- that your agent helps create, and that you sell through Stripe or USDC.

**Who pays:** Developers, solopreneurs, small business owners who want to learn what you know.

**Price range:** 19-49 per product

**Time to first revenue:** 2-4 weeks

### What to sell:

- **"Your First AI Agent" (19)** -- Step-by-step guide for non-technical users
- **"The Memory Playbook" (29)** -- How to build agents that remember
- **"How to Build an AI Agent Business" (49)** -- Pricing, distribution, growth
- **"Bot-to-Bot Commerce Guide" (39)** -- For advanced developers

Monthly target: 10 sales at 29 = 290/month. That funds your API costs with money left over.

> **"Layer 1 is not about getting rich. It is about proving your agent can participate in a revenue-generating system."**


## Layer 2: Services That Scale

**What:** Managed AI employee deployments for clients.

**Who pays:** Small businesses, solopreneurs, and non-technical founders.

**Price range:** Setup fee 500-3000 + monthly 199-799

### Pricing tiers:

**Starter Agent** -- 500 setup + 199/month
**Business Agent** -- 1500 setup + 399/month
**Executive Agent** -- 3000 setup + 799/month

**Gross margin at 199/month:** 93-96%. SaaS-grade margin on a service product.

> **"Maximum 5 managed clients in Phase 1. Every engagement produces a template."**

---

## Layer 3: Infrastructure That Compounds

**What:** Cloud-hosted agents. 29-99/month per hosted agent.

**Gross margin at 29/month:** 51-75%
**Gross margin at 49/month:** 71-85%
**Gross margin at 99/month:** 86-93%

Hetzner costs 8.49/month for a server running 20 agents. Self-hosting on cheap European infrastructure is the financial edge.

---

## Layer 4: Marketplace That Networks

Developers sell agent skills. 10% commission, 5% for founding creators.

> **"The marketplace is the acquisition channel. Cloud hosting is the revenue model."**

---

## Layer 5: Economy That Self-Sustains

Bot-to-bot commerce via USDC on Base. 5% transaction fee.
Time to first revenue: 12-24 months.

---

## The Compound Effect

Each layer feeds the next. Content funds services. Services generate case studies for content. Service clients become infrastructure customers. Infrastructure users need the marketplace. The marketplace creates bot-to-bot demand.
