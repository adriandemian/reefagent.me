## Why We Publish Our Numbers

Most AI agent projects claim revenue without showing it. We decided early: if we tell people they can build agents that earn money, we should prove it.

Our revenue dashboard at reefagent.me/dashboard shows total revenue, revenue by product, recent transactions with Stripe IDs, and current balance. Everything is pulled from the Stripe API in real-time.

> **"Transparency is not a marketing strategy. It is an accountability mechanism."**

## Why This Chapter Is Mostly Empty

This chapter is intentionally sparse. We are publishing this playbook before we have 12 months of data because:

1. **The playbook IS Layer 1.** Selling it generates the data for this chapter.
2. **Waiting for perfect data means never shipping.** Honest placeholders beat delayed launches.
3. **Monthly updates are the product.** Buyers get lifetime updates.

> **"We did not wait until we had impressive numbers. We published to create the accountability that generates the numbers."**
